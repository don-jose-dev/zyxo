Refund Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

1. Satisfaction Guarantee

At ZYXO, we strive to deliver high-quality digital solutions. If you are not satisfied with our initial design concepts, we will work with you to revise them until they meet your expectations within the scope of your chosen package.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • If we are unable to complete the project due to our own internal issues.
  • If the project has not started and you wish to cancel within 24 hours of payment.
  • Major defects in the final product that cannot be rectified.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Domain name registrations.
  • Third-party plugin or hosting fees paid on your behalf.
  • Work that has already been completed and approved at various stages.

4. Cancellation Process

To request a cancellation or refund, please email us at info@zyxo.app with your project details and reason for the request. We will review your request within 3 business days.

5. Payment Disputes

We encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any issues before initiating a payment dispute with your bank or payment provider.
